## Why retrieval remains a strategic layer · 為何檢索仍是策略層

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Large language models now offer very large context windows. That amplifies — not removes — the need for retrieval. Enterprise knowledge often exceeds billions of tokens. Injecting raw data into context is computationally wasteful, governance-risky, and operationally imprecise. Strategic retrieval ensures only relevant, permission-compliant, high-quality knowledge enters generation."
  zh="大型語言模型已有極長上下文窗口，這反而更突顯——而非削弱——檢索的必要。企業知識規模常達數十億 token；把原始資料整批塞進上下文既浪費運算、帶管治風險，亦難精準。策略性檢索確保只有相關、合權限、高質素知識進入生成。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Industry analysts report that a large share of enterprise generative AI initiatives require structured retrieval pipelines to mitigate hallucination and compliance risk. RAG architectures are safeguards, not optional extras."
  zh="業界分析顯示，不少企業生成式 AI 項目須依賴結構化檢索管道，以減輕幻覺與合規風險；RAG 架構是防線，而非可有可無的附加品。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="For organizations shaping an AI roadmap, retrieval design should align with enterprise strategy — not only developer convenience."
  zh="制定 AI 路線圖時，檢索設計應對齊企業策略，而非僅顧及開發便利。"
/>

## Naive RAG · 簡易 RAG（基礎架構）

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Naive RAG is the simplest pattern: vector embeddings and semantic similarity to retrieve top-k chunks before generation."
  zh="Naive RAG 是最簡模式：以向量嵌入與語意相似度在生成前檢索前 k 個文本塊。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="How it works: Queries and documents are embedded; cosine similarity finds closest chunks; retrieved content is appended to prompts; the LLM generates from augmented context."
  zh="運作方式：將查詢與文件嵌入；以餘弦相似度找最接近的塊；把檢索內容附加到提示；LLM 在增強上下文下生成。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Strengths: Rapid deployment with vector databases; fewer hallucinations when grounded in documents; low infrastructure complexity (no graph traversal or heavy orchestration)."
  zh="優點：可快速用向量庫上線；有文件依據時幻覺較少；基建簡單（無需圖遍歷或重型編排）。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Limitations: Semantic similarity is not always true relevance; no multi-hop reasoning; no self-correction if retrieval fails — generation may still proceed confidently."
  zh="限制：語意相近不等於真正相關；無多跳推理；檢索失敗亦未必自糾——生成仍可能看似很有把握。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Typical use cases: Internal documentation assistants, HR policy bots, customer knowledge portals, FAQ-style copilots."
  zh="典型場景：內部文件助理、人事政策機械人、客戶知識門戶、FAQ 式副駕。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Naive RAG is foundational — but insufficient for complex enterprise reasoning."
  zh="Naive RAG 是起點——但不足以應付複雜企業推理。"
/>

## Graph RAG · 圖譜 RAG（關係型智能）

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Vector similarity works at chunk level; enterprises often reason at relationship level: case law, supply chains, fraud chains, citation networks. Graph RAG adds structured knowledge."
  zh="向量相似度擅長塊級匹配；企業卻常在「關係」層面推理：判例、供應鏈、欺詐鏈、引用網絡。Graph RAG 引入結構化知識。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="How it works: Entities and relationships extracted (often via LLMs); knowledge graph built; community detection may cluster semantics; queries traverse paths; generation synthesizes multi-hop findings."
  zh="運作方式：抽取實體與關係（常借助 LLM）；建立知識圖譜；可用社群發現聚類語意；查詢沿路徑遍歷；生成綜合多跳發現。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Advantages: Multi-hop reasoning; holistic analysis of interconnected data; improved explainability via traceable paths."
  zh="優點：多跳推理；對互聯數據作整體分析；路徑可追溯，解釋性較佳。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Trade-offs: High indexing cost; ongoing maintenance for entity extraction and graph updates."
  zh="取捨：索引成本高；實體抽取與圖更新需持續維護。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Use cases: Legal research, pharma R&D knowledge graphs, compliance audit systems, M&A due diligence."
  zh="應用：法律研究、製藥研發知識圖譜、合規審計系統、併購盡職調查。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Graph RAG is transformative for relationship-intensive domains — not every workload needs it."
  zh="在關係密集的領域，Graph RAG 能帶來質變——但不是每個工作負載都需要。"
/>

## Hybrid RAG · 混合 RAG（企業生產標準）

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Real enterprise queries mix semantic and exact needs: policy names, contract IDs, IFRS references alongside conceptual questions. Hybrid RAG combines vector search with lexical search (e.g. BM25 / TF-IDF), sometimes with optional graph traversal."
  zh="真實企業查詢常混合語意與精準需求：政策名稱、合約編號、IFRS 條文與概念性問題並存。Hybrid RAG 結合向量與詞法搜尋（如 BM25／TF-IDF），有時可選配圖遍歷。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="How it works: Parallel vector + keyword retrieval; merge and re-rank; enriched context to the LLM; grounded response."
  zh="運作方式：並行向量與關鍵字檢索；合併與重排；向 LLM 提供豐富上下文；產出扎根於證據的回答。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Benefits: Fewer false negatives on exact terms; less semantic drift; higher recall and precision than single-method retrieval in many studies."
  zh="效益：專有名詞較少漏檢；語意漂移較小；多項研究顯示比單一方法有更高召回與精準度。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Infrastructure: Vector databases, search engines, ranking logic, monitoring. Complexity rises with reliability."
  zh="基建：向量庫、搜尋引擎、排序邏輯、監控；複雜度隨可靠性要求上升。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Use cases: Enterprise search, regulatory reporting assistants, IT operations knowledge, technical documentation copilots."
  zh="場景：企業搜尋、監管報告助理、IT 營運知識、技術文件副駕。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Hybrid RAG is production-grade, not experimental."
  zh="Hybrid RAG 屬生產級，而非實驗性質。"
/>

### Hybrid vs vector-only · 混合與純向量

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Vector-only RAG struggles with exact keyword compliance, structured references, policy numbers, and contract identifiers. Hybrid captures both semantic meaning and exact terminology — critical in legal, financial, and regulatory settings."
  zh="純向量 RAG 在精準關鍵字合規、結構化引用、政策編號與合約識別上較弱。混合模式同時涵蓋語意與精準用語——在法律、金融與監管場景至關重要。"
/>

### When graph beats hybrid · 何時圖譜勝過混合

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Hybrid improves retrieval. Graph transforms reasoning. Prefer Graph RAG when insights depend on entity relationships, multi-hop logic, or interconnected records — e.g. fraud ownership networks, legal precedent chains, supply chain risk, M&A diligence. If the task is how entities relate, not only which text matches, graph approaches become strategically necessary."
  zh="混合強化「檢索」；圖譜改變「推理」。當洞見取決於實體關係、多跳邏輯或互聯紀錄——例如詐欺持股網絡、判例鏈、供應鏈風險、併購盡職——應優先考慮 Graph RAG。若任務是實體「如何關聯」，而非僅哪段文字匹配，圖方法在策略上屬必要。"
/>

## Contextual RAG · 情境化 RAG（保留跨塊語境）

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Traditional chunking can break pronoun references, section continuity, and nuance. Contextual RAG enriches chunks with document-level metadata."
  zh="傳統分塊易打斷代詞指涉、章節連貫與細微語氣。Contextual RAG 以文件級元數據豐富各塊。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="How it works: Analyze structure; attach section headers and positional context; enrich embeddings with semantic role; retrieve context-aware chunks."
  zh="運作方式：分析結構；附加小節標題與位置語境；以語意角色豐富嵌入；檢索具情境感的塊。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Enterprise impact: Better disambiguation; stronger long-form document handling; reduced context drift — valuable in regulated, medical, and legal documents."
  zh="企業效益：消歧更佳；長文件處理更穩；減少語境漂移——對受規管、醫療與法律文件特別有價值。"
/>

## Adaptive RAG · 自適應 RAG（按複雜度調策略）

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Static pipelines waste resources: a one-line policy question and a cross-regional contract analysis should not follow identical paths. Adaptive RAG selects retrieval depth from query classification."
  zh="靜態管道浪費資源：一句政策問答與跨區合約分析不應走同一條路。Adaptive RAG 依查詢分類決定檢索深度。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="How it works: Classify complexity; route simple queries to fast paths; route complex queries to multi-step pipelines; optimize latency and cost."
  zh="運作方式：分類複雜度；簡單查詢走快路徑；複雜查詢走多步管道；優化延遲與成本。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Benefits: Cost optimization; low latency for operational questions; depth where strategy requires it."
  zh="效益：成本優化；營運問題低延遲；策略需要處才加深。"
/>

## Agentic RAG · 代理式 RAG（由檢索到自主推理）

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Traditional RAG retrieves then generates. Agentic RAG adds planning, tool use, evaluation, and refinement — supporting multi-step workflows, cross-system access, iterative reasoning, and memory."
  zh="傳統 RAG 先檢索再生成。Agentic RAG 加入規劃、工具使用、評估與修正——支援多步流程、跨系統存取、迭代推理與記憶。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="How it works: Task decomposition (e.g. ReAct-style or chain-of-thought orchestration); tools hit vectors, SQL, APIs, storage, graphs; short- and long-term memory; iterative refinement until confidence thresholds."
  zh="運作方式：任務分解（如 ReAct 或思維鏈編排）；工具觸及向量、SQL、API、儲存、圖；短長期記憶；迭代修正直至達信心門檻。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Advantages: Cross-system intelligence (CRM, tickets, databases, policies); autonomous research for investigations, compliance, root-cause analysis; higher analytical quality on complex tasks."
  zh="優點：跨系統智能（CRM、工單、庫、政策）；調查、合規、根因分析等自主研究；複雜任務分析質素較高。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Governance note: Agentic patterns increase governance exposure due to autonomous tool usage. They are strategic infrastructure — not the default for every use case."
  zh="管治提示：代理自主調用工具會提高管治曝露；屬策略基建——並非每個場景的預設。"
/>

## Self-RAG · 自省式 RAG（以反思換可靠）

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Standard RAG can still produce confident wrong answers. Self-RAG adds reflection: evaluate whether retrieval is needed, assess relevance, generate with self-assessment, and iterate retrieval or regeneration when confidence is low."
  zh="標準 RAG 仍可能自信地答錯。Self-RAG 加入反思：判斷是否需檢索、評估相關性、生成時自我評估，並在信心不足時重檢索或再生。"
/>

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Enterprise value: Fewer unsupported claims; confidence signals for explainability; retrieval only when necessary."
  zh="企業價值：較少無據主張；可輸出信心訊號以利解釋；必要時才檢索。"
/>

## Modular RAG · 模組化 RAG（為演進而設計）

<BilingualParagraph
  en="Monolithic RAG ages quickly as models and strategies change. Modular RAG treats indexing, retrieval, generation, and orchestration as composable blocks: pluggable vector, keyword, graph, or SQL retrieval; upgrade LLMs without rewiring indexes; central orchestration."
  zh="單體 RAG 隨模型與策略變化迅速老化。模組化 RAG 把索引、檢索、生成與編排當成可組件：向量、關鍵字、圖或 SQL 檢索可插拔；升級 LLM 不必重接索引；中央編排協調。"

<BilingualParagraph
